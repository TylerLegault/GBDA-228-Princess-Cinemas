The Odeon Gallery

The Odeon Gallery is a fine little exhibition space in the basement of the Princess Twin Cinemas featuring 100 year old maple floors and 4 big walls in a 510 square foot space! The walls feature a chain hanging system with 's' hooks and is flexible for all sizes of pieces. We exhibit local artists, hosting one month and 2 month long shows. Shows are booked well in advance. The gallery is a secure place to show your work. Thousands of people pass through the cinemas each month and visit the Odeon. Princess provides the space, lighting & will assist with promotion. The artist can book a small catered "opening" with the Princess Cafe. Contact Marc in the Cafe at 519 886-0227. We do not charge a commission if a work sells. Our monthly charge for renting the gallery for an exhibition is $150.00 plus HST.
